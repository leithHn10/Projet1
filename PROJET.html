Who is Santa

My name is Leith, also known as Santa. I'm 19 years old and recently earned my high school diploma. I’m passionate about coding, design, and creating online projects especially in the e-commerce field. My journey into web development began when I discovered an announcement for the GoMyCode Summer Academy. I started researching more about it, and that inspired me to begin learning HTML and dive into the world of web development. My long-term goal is to create my own tech company. Outside of coding, I enjoy graphic design and managing my online store, "The Futures", where I explore entrepreneurship and creativity.

What is The Futures

The Futures Store is a digital design shop launched in 2020 during the global COVID-19 lockdown. It was founded by Leith at just 13 years old, driven by a passion for design, gaming culture, and digital creativity. What began as a personal project during confinement quickly evolved into a thriving online store serving creators across the Middle East. We specialize in graphic design products for content creators, especially streamers, gamers, podcasters, and influencers. Our designs are crafted to help creators build strong visual identities from stream overlays, panels, and logo kits to digital templates and branding assets.
